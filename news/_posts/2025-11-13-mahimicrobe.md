title: Pipes Lab wins MahiMicrobe 2025 Award
author: Pipes Lab
date: 2025-11-12

Congratulations to **Stephanie Murray**, a Ph.D. student in the Pipes Lab, who was awarded the **MahiMicrobe 2025 Michael Bruno Award for Excellence in Microbiome Science - Graduate Research Award** from the *Center for Microbiome Analysis through Island Knowledge and Investigation (C-MĀIKI)*.  

Her project, *Identifying the Protective Microbiome of Rapid ʻŌhiʻa Death Survivors*, was recognized for addressing a critical Hawaiʻi-based conservation challenge through a microbiome-focused solution.  

This award provides $7,000 in research funding and invites Stephanie to present her work at the upcoming **C-MĀIKI Symposium (Spring 2026)**.
